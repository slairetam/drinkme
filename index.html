Counting You

I am a shifting shape
a spectral salt ocean
mineral red from a fountain star

when i explode i waterfall
i split into you
and stay

when i look, you are there
when i look again, i'm gone

in two places, one of us
where I begin
where you leave off

Something whirring
Something still turns

yesterday, I was walking
by myself across some world .



Something I'm Not

My way, your way
I clamor
While I'm spinning

Then run
in a clatter, all particles
and no grace

I jump and fall
I leap from a plane in a twist
I'm unbound and I'm reacting
I am in a race
or jettisoned, I'm flailing

I see the middle only
and no end
all the trees can fall to pieces
all the clouds spire
flowers crying
in my smirk
This confusion is such a spell
and now I can't see the bottom

I'm not what I should have been
I became someone else
Smoking, and seething, how I missed myself

I jumped too soon
but it was no jump
A machine, I tumbled Fast.
